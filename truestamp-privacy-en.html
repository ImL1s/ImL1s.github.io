Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 26, 2025

TrueStamp Timestamp Camera (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is developed and operated by ImL1s (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "us"). We highly value your privacy, and this Privacy Policy aims to explain how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. About the App

This App is a professional timestamp camera application that automatically adds time, location, GPS coordinates, weather, and other information as watermarks when taking photos. It is suitable for construction site documentation, insurance claims evidence, news reporting, and other scenarios requiring proof of time.

2. Data Collection and Use

To provide complete functionality and services, this App may collect or use the following information:

  • Account Data: If you choose to use Google Sign-In or Apple Sign-In, we will obtain your basic account information (such as email address and display name) through Firebase Authentication to provide account synchronization and cloud backup features.
  • Location Data: This App requires access to your location to display address and GPS coordinates in photo watermarks. Location information is only read when taking photos and is not continuously tracked.
  • Camera & Photos: This App requires access to the camera for taking photos and access to the photo library to save or read photos. Photo data is only stored locally on your device or in cloud storage of your choice.
  • Weather Data: If you enable the weather watermark feature, the App will send queries to third-party weather APIs based on your location to retrieve current weather information.
  • Team & Project Data: If you use the team feature, your team information, project data, and related photos will be stored in Firebase Firestore and Firebase Storage to provide team collaboration and data synchronization features.
  • Device & Log Data: When the App encounters errors or crashes, we may collect crash-related log data (via Google Firebase Crashlytics), including device model, operating system version, and time of error occurrence, to help fix issues.
  • Usage Data: We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data (such as page views and feature usage frequency) to analyze user behavior and optimize the App experience.

3. Third-Party Services

This App integrates or uses the following third-party services, which may collect and process your data according to their privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Analytics, Crashlytics): For account verification, data storage, photo storage, data analysis, and error reporting.
  • Google Maps / Geocoding API: For obtaining address information and map functionality.
  • Weather API: For obtaining weather information.
  • NTP Time Server: For calibrating accurate time.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may use device identifiers and other information to provide personalized ads.
  • RevenueCat: For managing in-app subscription services.
  • Google Play Services: For Google Sign-In and app updates.
  • Sign in with Apple: For Apple Sign-In functionality (iOS platform only).

4. Digital Signature and Anti-Tampering

This App provides digital signature functionality to add anti-tampering signatures to your photos. Signature keys are stored locally on your device (using secure storage mechanisms) and are not uploaded to our servers.

5. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information security and take reasonable technical measures to prevent unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure. Your data is encrypted during transmission and storage through Firebase's security mechanisms. However, please note that Internet transmission cannot be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete your account and related data at any time within the App. After account deletion, your cloud data will be permanently removed. If you need assistance deleting data, please contact us using the contact information below.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13 years of age and does not intentionally collect personal information from children.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We recommend that you periodically review this page for the latest information. Changes to the policy become effective immediately upon posting on this page.
