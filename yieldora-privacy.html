Overview

Yieldora is a cryptocurrency lending automation app for Bitfinex. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information.

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

Information We Do NOT Collect

Yieldora is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device information
  • Biometric data

API Credentials

To connect with Bitfinex, you need to provide your API credentials:

  • API credentials are stored locally on your device only
  • Credentials are encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • We never transmit your credentials to our servers
  • All communication with Bitfinex happens directly from your device

Third-Party Services

Bitfinex API

Yieldora connects directly to Bitfinex's API for lending operations. Please review Bitfinex's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data.

RevenueCat (In-App Purchases)

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat collects minimal data required for purchase processing. Please review RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

AdMob (Advertising)

The free tier displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device and usage data for ad personalization. Please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • All sensitive data is encrypted on your device
  • Secure communication protocols (HTTPS/WSS)
  • No cloud storage of credentials
  • Local-only data storage

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete from our servers. You can delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data.

Children's Privacy

Yieldora is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
