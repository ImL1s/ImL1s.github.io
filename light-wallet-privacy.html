Light Wallet

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Light Wallet is a browser extension that allows users to manage Ethereum-based cryptocurrency. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

We do not collect any personal data.

Data Collection

Light Wallet operates entirely on your local device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Store any data on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using your browser's secure storage:

  • Private Keys & Seed Phrases: Encrypted and stored only on your device. They never leave your browser.
  • Wallet Settings: Network preferences and settings are stored locally.
  • Transaction History: Fetched from public blockchain explorers and not stored by us.

Third-Party Services

Light Wallet interacts with the following external services:

  1. Ethereum RPC Providers: To query blockchain data and submit transactions
  2. Block Explorers (Etherscan, etc.): To fetch transaction history
  3. CoinGecko API: To fetch current cryptocurrency prices

These services receive only the necessary blockchain data (wallet addresses, transaction data) and are subject to their own privacy policies.

Security

  • All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard cryptography
  • Private keys never leave your device
  • Auto-lock feature protects against unauthorized access

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. All your data remains under your control on your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
