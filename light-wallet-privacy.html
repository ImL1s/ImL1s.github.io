Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using your browser's secure storage:

  • Private Keys & Seed Phrases: Encrypted and stored only on your device. They never leave your browser.
  • Wallet Settings: Network preferences and settings are stored locally.
  • Transaction History: Fetched from public blockchain explorers, not stored by us.

Third-Party Services

Light Wallet interacts with the following external services:

  1. Ethereum RPC Providers: To query blockchain data and submit transactions
  2. Block Explorers (Etherscan, etc.): To fetch transaction history
  3. CoinGecko API: To fetch current cryptocurrency prices

These services receive only necessary blockchain data (wallet addresses, transaction data) and are subject to their own privacy policies.
