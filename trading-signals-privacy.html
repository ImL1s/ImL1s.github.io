Trading Signals Bot

Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Trading Signals Bot is a mobile application that provides trading signals, market news, AI-powered chart analysis, and portfolio tracking. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Your privacy is important to us. We only collect data necessary to provide our services and improve your experience.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in using Firebase Authentication, we collect:

  • Email address: Used for account identification and communication
  • Display name: Used to personalize your experience
  • User ID: A unique identifier for your account

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the app:

  • Watchlist: Symbols you add to track
  • Notification preferences: Your push notification settings
  • AI analysis usage: Number of AI analyses performed (for usage limits)
  • Subscription status: Whether you have an active Pro subscription

Device Information

For push notifications and analytics, we may collect:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Push notification token
  • App version

How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Account management Email, User ID
Personalized watchlist Symbol preferences
Push notifications Device token, notification settings
AI analysis limits Usage count
Subscription management Subscription status
Ad personalization Device information (if ads enabled)

Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: User authentication
  • Cloud Firestore: Data storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Push notifications
  • Firebase Remote Config: App configuration

See Firebase Privacy Policy

Google Gemini AI

Used for AI-powered chart analysis. Chart images and analysis requests are processed by Google's AI services.

See Google AI Terms of Service

RevenueCat

Manages in-app subscriptions. Collects purchase history and subscription status.

See RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

Displays advertisements to free users. May collect device information for ad personalization.

See Google Privacy Policy

News Data Providers

We aggregate news from multiple sources:

  • Finnhub
  • Marketaux
  • CryptoCompare

News data is cached on our servers and does not include your personal information.

Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely in Google Cloud Firestore
  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device
  • We do not store your payment information (handled by Apple/Google)

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Usage history: Retained for 90 days
  • AI analysis cache: Retained for 24 hours

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications or personalized ads

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Financial Disclaimer: This app provides trading signals and analysis for informational purposes only. It does not constitute financial advice. Always do your own research before making investment decisions.
